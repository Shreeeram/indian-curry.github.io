Indian Curry: Functional programming served red hot.

Posted on August 29, 2016

Indian Curry is informal group of functional programming enthusiasts loosely based in India. In the global software world, India is already a big player. We believe that if the industry has to move forward in terms of building large and reliable software, functional programming and related formal methods have to be adopted in a large scale. The goal is to accelerate this process.

Who can join

Any one who is involved in functional programming are welcome.
